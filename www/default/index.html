About me

My name is Yordan Panayotov Yordanov and I was born in Straldzha, Bulgaria in 1962. I teach "Technology" in the special "Carving" at the National Art Academy in Sofia, Bulgaria. Since 1996 I am a member of the "Union of Bulgarian Artists" section "Carving". I finished my higher education in 1996 at the National Academy of Arts in Sofia, Bulgaria specialization "Carving" under Prof. V.Lilov, and my secondary education in Secondary Special Art School in Kotel, Bulgaria special "Carving" in 1981.

Wood sculpture is my specialty and passion. I have a large number of participations in numerous exhibitions both in Bulgaria and in countries such as Belgium and France."
